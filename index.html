How to play:

Guess the right DRAG QUEEN in 8 tries.

Each guess must be a valid contestant from RuPaul's Drag Race and its international versions. Queens from all Drag Race franchises around the world are valid.

Type your guess and hit "Guess". After each guess, you'll recieve clues based on the queen you chose, showing you how close you are from the right anwser. You'll be able to guess based on season, age, country and type of drag.

Examples
The queen is younger and from an earlier Drag Race season than your guess.

The queen is also a winner and a Fashion Queen, but is from another franchise.

A new drag queen will be available each day for you to guess!
